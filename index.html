Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the conference?

Developers, engineers, students, founders, researchers, and technology enthusiasts are welcome to attend DevConf 2026.

Up to what age is registration free?

Children aged 12 years and below may attend free when accompanied by a registered adult.

Will recordings be available?

Yes. Every attendee receives access to all recorded sessions for 30 days after the conference.

How do I check in?

Simply present your confirmation email or QR code at the registration desk.
